# Blood Hunter Class Features

## Level 1 Features

### Hunter's Bane (1st level)

You have survived the Hunter's Bane—a dangerous, long-guarded ritual that alters your life's blood, forever binding you to the darkness and honing your senses against it.

**Benefits:**
- Advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track fey, fiends, or undead
- Advantage on Intelligence checks to recall information about fey, fiends, or undead
- Empowers your body to control and shape hemocraft magic

### Blood Maledict (1st level)

You gain the ability to channel—or sometimes sacrifice—a part of your vital essence to curse and manipulate creatures through hemocraft magic.

**Mechanics:**
- Learn 1 Blood Curse at 1st level
- Learn additional curses at 6th, 10th, 14th, and 18th level
- Can replace one known curse when learning a new one
- **Uses:** 1/rest (2/rest at 6th, 3/rest at 13th, 4/rest at 17th)

**Amplifying Curses:**
- Before the curse affects the target, you can amplify it
- Take necrotic damage equal to one hemocraft die (cannot be reduced)
- Amplified curses gain additional effects
- Creatures without blood are immune to curses UNLESS amplified

## Level 2 Features

### Fighting Style (2nd level)

Choose one fighting style:

**Archery**
- +2 bonus to attack rolls with ranged weapons

**Dueling**
- +2 to damage rolls when wielding a melee weapon in one hand with no other weapons

**Great Weapon Fighting**
- Reroll 1s and 2s on non-rite damage dice with two-handed/versatile weapons
- Must use the new roll

**Two-Weapon Fighting**
- Add ability modifier to damage of the second attack

### Crimson Rite (2nd level)

You learn to invoke a rite of hemocraft that infuses your weapon strikes with elemental energy.

**Activation:**
- Bonus action to activate on one weapon you're holding
- Take necrotic damage equal to one hemocraft die (cannot be reduced)
- Lasts until you finish a short or long rest

**Effects:**
- Attacks with the weapon are magical
- Deal extra damage equal to your hemocraft die of the rite's type
- Only one rite can be active on a weapon at a time
- Other creatures can't benefit from your rite

**Rites Known:**
- Learn 1 rite at 2nd level
- Learn additional rites at 7th and 14th level

## Level 3 Feature

### Blood Hunter Order (3rd level)

You commit to an order of blood hunters whose philosophy will guide you throughout your life.

**Orders Available:**
- Ghostslayer
- Lycan
- Mutant
- Profane Soul

**Progression:** Gain order features at 3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th, and 18th level.

## Level 4+ Features

### Ability Score Improvement (4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, 19th level)

- Increase one ability score by 2, OR
- Increase two ability scores by 1 each
- Maximum 20 in any score
- Can take a feat instead (if using optional feats rule)

### Extra Attack (5th level)

You can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action on your turn.

### Brand of Castigation (6th level)

When you damage a creature with a weapon for which you have an active crimson rite, you can channel hemocraft magic to sear an arcane brand into that creature (no action required).

**Effects:**
- Always know the direction to the branded creature (same plane only)
- When the branded creature deals damage to you or a creature within 5 feet of you, it takes psychic damage equal to your Hemocraft modifier (minimum 1)

**Duration & Limits:**
- Lasts until dismissed or you brand another creature
- Can be dispelled with *Dispel Magic* (spell level = half your blood hunter level, max 9th)
- **Uses:** 1/short rest

### Grim Psychometry (9th level)

You gain a supernatural talent for discerning the secrets surrounding mysterious relics or places touched by evil.

**Benefit:**
- Advantage on Intelligence (History) checks to recall information about sinister or tragic history of an object you're touching or your current location
- High rolls may grant brief visions of the past (DM's discretion)

### Dark Augmentation (10th level)

The magic of hemocraft suffuses your body to permanently reinforce your resilience.

**Benefits:**
- Speed increases by 5 feet
- Bonus to Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution saving throws equal to your Hemocraft modifier (minimum +1)

### Brand of Tethering (13th level)

Your Brand of Castigation becomes more powerful.

**Improvements:**
- Psychic damage increases to twice your Hemocraft modifier (minimum 2)
- Branded creature can't take the Dash action
- If branded creature attempts to teleport or leave current plane:
  - Takes 4d6 psychic damage
  - Must make a Wisdom saving throw
  - On failure, the attempt fails

### Hardened Soul (14th level)

You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed and frightened.

### Sanguine Mastery (20th level)

Your mastery of blood magic reaches its height, mitigating your sacrifice and empowering your expertise.

**Benefits:**
- Once per turn, when a feature requires you to roll a hemocraft die, you can reroll and use either result
- Whenever you score a critical hit with a weapon for which you have an active crimson rite, regain one expended use of Blood Maledict
