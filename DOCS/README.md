## Quick Reference

### Key Mechanics

**Hemocraft Die**
- 1d4 (levels 1-4)
- 1d6 (levels 5-10)
- 1d8 (levels 11-16)
- 1d10 (levels 17-20)

**Hemocraft Save DC**
`8 + proficiency bonus + Hemocraft modifier (Intelligence or Wisdom)`

**Blood Maledict Uses**
- 1/rest (levels 1-5)
- 2/rest (levels 6-12)
- 3/rest (levels 13-16)
- 4/rest (levels 17-20)

### Class Identity

Blood hunters sacrifice their own vitality (hit points) to fuel powerful abilities:
- **Crimson Rites:** Enhance weapons with elemental damage (costs 1 hemocraft die in HP)
- **Blood Curses:** Manipulate creatures through blood magic (amplification costs 1 hemocraft die in HP)
- **Resource Management:** Balance offensive power with survivability
